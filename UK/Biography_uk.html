Born in 1980 in Mondragone, while studying to become a surveyor, he approached music by starting to play the violin. Fascinated by the stylistic, geometric and acoustic perfection of the instrument, he begins his luthier career at the school of violin making “Liutaio ed Archettaio” in Gubbio. Having decided to deepen his knowledge, he consolidates the construction techniques, and grows passionate for the art of restoration while at the “Violin Making and Repair School” in Newark (Great Britain). After graduation he has the opportunity to have several work experiences abroad, working with Frederik Beker in Montpellier, and Hans Weisshaar in Los Angeles. In 2008 he arrives in Cremona, where he collaborates with several workshops, developing his own style inspired by the great violin makers of the past, and by the construction method based on the Golden Section. In 2015 he opens his own business in Cremona.
